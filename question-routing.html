// Intelligent intake

It doesn't guess the gaps. It asks the right person.

When the framework can't resolve something from the input, it doesn't hallucinate an answer or bury it in a TODO. It generates a precise question and routes it to the role who actually owns that answer.

So technical documentation gets fully filled out by the right people — and execs stay out of the weeds instead of being asked questions they shouldn't have to answer.
